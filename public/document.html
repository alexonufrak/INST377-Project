Information Problem

Choosing a place to live is a very big deal for everyone. For that reason there are many factors that people consider when deciding to settle in a place. One of the important factors is safety issues and emergency services available in the area. Homes near fire stations reduce those if not eliminate them. Houses near the fire stations can get faster emergency services and also there are less likely for crime to occur as there are people moving around all day. Our project will allow people to locate fire stations near them which they could use to determine which houses are close to the stations and which houses are not. It will also provide information on whether or not medical and ambulance service are available at that station and the type of station, volunteer or state run. Also knowing the location of the fire stations near their neighborhood could help people find shelter during disasters and where to seek help during emergency situations.

The stakeholder for this project are people living in PG county as they would be able to see out help and or shelter during emergency time. New residents coming to PG county seeking for new residents can decide which house is the best when it comes to living near fire stations.

People who are thinking about coming to live in PG county can use our system to figure out a place that could be viewed as a safe place to live without wasting too much time searching around or asking other people. Also people living in PG county could use our system to figure a place to seek emergency help and also find shelter during emergency time. We can rate the stations based on which ones provide both medical and ambulance services and the type of station, giving higher rank for state and county over volunteer.
